2. Technical Tools

a. Welding Control and Reporting System

WCRS is a development data for estimation, or other data for developing welding related activities. Easy monitoring status of welding activity fit-up, welding execution date, welding electrode used, welder who weld the joint, visual welding etc ), non-destructive testing (PT, MT, UT & RT), preheat & Post Weld Heat Treatment and other relevant testing used such as hardness test & positive material identification. WCRS Making fast traceability of the relevant welding data required by site management such as welding productivity, welder performance, status of nondestructive test, preheat / PWHT, etc.
